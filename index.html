About Us

Launched on International Day Against Homophobia, Transphobia, and Biphobia (IDAHOTB) in 2022, Equality and Pride for Indo-Caribbeans EPIC is a bold initiative dedicated to uplifting LGBTQ2S+ Indo-Caribbean voices. Proudly presented by the Indo-Caribbean Canadian Association (ICCA), EPIC stands as a first-of-its-kind platform created by and for our community.

On a day that calls for action against hate, violence, and discrimination targeting diverse sexual orientations and gender identities, we proudly introduced a standalone initiative committed to building safer spaces for Indo-Caribbean LGBTQ2S+ individuals across Canada.

We invite you to join us—as allies, advocates, and community members—to listen, share stories, break down barriers, and celebrate love in all its beautiful forms.

📘 A Guide to Coming Out

On National Coming Out Day (October 11, 2023), EPIC released the first edition of A Coming Out Guide for Canada’s Indo-Caribbean Community—a heartfelt resource for queer and questioning individuals and their loved ones. You can explore it here.

🎉 Culture, Celebration & Change

Since its launch, EPIC has brought vibrant programming to the Indo-Caribbean LGBTQ2S+ community, including our annual Wotless Event every Pride Month in Toronto. It’s a joyful, affirming space where we celebrate our culture through food, dance, and connection—something not always accessible in traditional Caribbean spaces.

Looking ahead, our upcoming Mash Up De Closet event in 2025 blends education with celebration, pushing forward the work of decolonization and cultural pride. Through initiatives like these, we aim to create spaces where everyone can show up fully, safely, and joyfully.
