About Us

Launched on International Day Against Homophobia, Transphobia, and Biphobia (IDAHOTB) in 2022, Equality and Pride for Indo-Caribbeans EPIC is a bold initiative dedicated to uplifting LGBTQ2S+ Indo-Caribbean and BIPOC voices. Proudly presented by the Indo-Caribbean Canadian Association (ICCA), EPIC stands as a first-of-its-kind platform created by and for our community.

On a day that calls for action against hate, violence, and discrimination targeting diverse sexual orientations and gender identities, we proudly introduced a standalone initiative committed to building safer spaces for Indo-Caribbean LGBTQ2S+ individuals across Canada.
